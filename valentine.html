My darling Troy

In the city of legends, where echoes still call,
Love stood unshaken, beyond any wall.
Not even a thousand ships, nor the embers of war,
Could burn what the heart had cherished before.

My love, like the walls of that fabled place,
Stands tall and eternal, time cannot erase.
No wooden horse, no cunning art,
Could breach the fortress of my heart.

So here I stand, with words as my sword,
Not to conquer, but to adore.
My darling Troy, forever you'll be,
A kingdom of love inside of me.
